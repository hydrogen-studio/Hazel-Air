1.0 Preamble

1.1 What is HZA

Hazel Air Virtual, often abbreviated as HZA, is a Virtual Airline that have a passenger division and a charter division. HZA is independent and is not associated and related to any real world airlines, nor does HZA provides real world services.

1.2 Goal

Hazel Air Virtual aims to provide its pilots with the most unique, realistic, and fun services.

2.0 Memberships

2.1 Membership Agreements

HZA uses vAMSYS as our crew resources, therefore our pilots are required to follow the vAMSYS policies. 

2.2 Membership Requirements

1. At least 16 years old - need parental permission if under 18.

2. Have not created another account with us - Email hazelairhelp@gmail.com to reinstate closed accounts.

3. Must be mature enough to communicate in discussion forums.

2.3 Membership Registration

Membership could be started for free by clicking on Membership Registration at the top of the Navigation Bar.

2.4 Transfer Hours

At this moment, we are not allowing any transfer hours and points to be made outside of our partner virtual airlines. Transferable hours and points from partner virtual airlines are transferred 1 to 1.

2.5 Membership Terminations

Membership could be terminated with any of the following reasons: Failed Activity Check, Decision by HZA Staff, Inappropriate Behaviors. Terminated memberships are eligible for reinstatement upon an email to hazelairhelp@gmail.com. All reinstatement decision are made by HZA Management.

2.6 Behavior Expectations

Our Pilots are expected to: 

1. Be respectful - Disrespectful behavior is not tolerated, as we want everyone to feel included.

2. No offensive behavior - Arguments are allowed as long as it is appropriate and each party is respectful. Offensive behaviors will not be tolerated.

3. Abuse - Pilots are not allowed to misuse any HZA certified systems.

3.0 Flights

3.1 Flight Callsign

All flights should be completed in the following callsigns

1. HZA - HA - Hazel Air - Most flights are done with HZA, being our main airline branch.

2. HZC - HC - Hazel Air Charter - Our charter flights division, used for charter flights.

3.2 Flight Tracking

All flights should be tracked with Pegasus, our ACARS flight tracking software. Documentation could be found under resources tab of our Pilot Portal.

3.3 Dispatch

You need at least 20 minutes or have received the load sheet before you can leave gate/ramp, whichever came later. This is to allow realistic simulation of turnaround, boarding, and refueling.

3.4 Scoring and Ranks

All of our PIREPs are scored based on a internal procedure, which based on the result of the scoring, points and flight hours will be credited to the pilot's account. Once a certain points and hours requirement are met, the pilot will rank up automatically.

3.5 Aircraft Matching

All flights aircraft should have a matching name to the filed aircraft. For example, if the filed aircraft is A320, then the aircraft flown by the pilot must be an A320ceo. If the filed aircraft is A359, the flown aircraft must be A350-900. Unmatching Airframes/Liveries will cause the PIREP to be sent for review, which a staff member will decide the result of the PIREP.
